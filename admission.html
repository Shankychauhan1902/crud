Admission Criteria

  • BCA:

    10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks. Preference to students with a computer background.

  • BBA:

    10+2 or equivalent in any stream with a minimum of 50% marks. An entrance exam may be required.

  • LLB:

    Graduation in any stream with a minimum of 45% marks. Entrance test and interview will be conducted.

  • BAJMC:

    10+2 or equivalent with at least 50% marks. An aptitude for media and communications is a plus.

  • MCA:

    Bachelor's degree with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at graduation level, with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.

  • MAJMC:

    Graduation in any stream with a minimum of 50% marks. Prior experience in media or journalism can be beneficial.

  • BALLB:

    10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 45% marks. Entrance examination score and interview performance will be considered.
